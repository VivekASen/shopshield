ShopShield — Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 17, 2025

Summary

ShopShield is a local-first browser extension that helps users pause before making online purchases. By default it only stores data on your device and does not transmit any personal information or payment data to any server.

Data collected & stored

  • Override logs: When you choose to override the pause modal we optionally prompt you to add a short note. That note is stored locally in your browser's extension storage (chrome.storage.local). The log includes timestamp, page URL, and the note you entered. We never collect or store credit card numbers or other sensitive payment details.
  • Settings: Your configuration (enabled/disabled, delay time, whitelist) is stored locally.

What we never do

  • We do NOT read, store, or transmit credit card numbers, CVV, expiration dates, or any payment form fields.
  • We do NOT transmit your browsing history or page content to any server.
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking by default.

Permissions

ShopShield requests the following permissions in the browser: storage, activeTab, and scripting. These are needed to detect checkout UI on pages and to inject the pause modal. On the Chrome Web Store we recommend using minimal host permissions — we will request access only to domains you allow (or limit to common shopping domains).
